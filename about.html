The Architect

"We didn't build a mall. We designed a gallery for life, where the building itself recedes to let the products and people become the focal art."

The Visionary

Julian Vane

Recognized for his minimalist approach, Vane created a mathematical balance between space and structural integrity. Built with a focus on seamless connectivity and comfort, the mall is divided into distinct, well-planned zones including the North Gallery, the South Pavilion, and the iconic Lumen Skywalk.

Silent Luxury: Luxury shouldn't shout. Our design utilizes monochromatic tones and raw materials—brushed steel, volcanic stone, and reinforced glass—to create a backdrop that exudes confidence through restraint.
